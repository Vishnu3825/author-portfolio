About the Author

I write myth-inspired fiction with an emotional core — stories that blend epic scale and intimate human stakes. My debut (and current) book, Asuryan, is available on Amazon.

My writing explores memory, morality, and the small choices that become legends. I invite you to read, question, and travel with me into the places where myth meets the human heart.
